**Brief Connect 2.6 strengthens the foundations of the platform while putting more control in the hands of administrators and support teams.**

**What this means for customers:** more self-service for administrators, clearer visibility into permissions, fewer incidents, a more stable day-to-day experience, and a more flexible platform underneath.
