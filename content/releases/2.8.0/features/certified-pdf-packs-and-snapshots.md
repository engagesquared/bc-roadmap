---
title: "Certified PDF Packs and Snapshots"
summary: "Replace owner-password restrictions with certified PDFs so packs and snapshots open cleanly in supported viewers while remaining tamper-evident."
---

Replace owner-password-only protection on PDF Packs and Snapshots with certified PDFs so recipients can open them without password prompts in supported viewers.

This keeps the point-in-time, immutable-artifact intent of generated packs while making later edits detectable through certificate and signature status in standard PDF clients.
