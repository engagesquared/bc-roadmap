**Brief Connect 3.0 will be a major rethink of the platform's user experience and design language.**

Our UX north star is that Brief Connect should be so intuitive, even a new user fresh to the public service can use it confidently on day one.

We will be undertaking a deep dive into how people use Brief Connect day-to-day, co-designed with users through a human-centred design process and informed directly by their feedback. The goal is a refreshed visual identity and interaction model that reflects real workflows and pain points.

This release will also explore what it means to design a UX in the era of AI. We will be looking at how modern AI and agentic systems can be woven into the application experience to enhance productivity and deliver tangible efficiency gains for our customers.

**What this means for customers:** a better day-to-day experience, designed with users rather than just for them, and a platform that is ready to take full advantage of what AI makes possible.
